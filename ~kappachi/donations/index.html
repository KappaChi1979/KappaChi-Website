Ways to Support

Scholarship Fund

Support educational opportunities for deserving students. Your contribution helps provide financial assistance to brothers maintaining academic excellence while giving back to the community.

Brotherhood Development

Fund leadership training, mentorship programs, and character development initiatives that strengthen our bonds and prepare brothers for lifelong success.

Community Service

Enable impactful community service projects including food drives, educational programs, and neighborhood improvement initiatives that embody our motto "Service For Humanity."

Chapter House & Facilities

Support maintenance, improvements, and equipment for our chapter facilities, ensuring a welcoming environment for meetings, events, and brotherhood activities.
