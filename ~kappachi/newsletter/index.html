Annual Thanksgiving Food Drive Exceeds Goal

The Kappa Chi Chapter successfully collected over 500 pounds of food and $1,200 in donations for local families in need this Thanksgiving. Our brothers partnered with the East Tennessee Food Bank to distribute meals to 75 families in the Knoxville area.

Brotherhood Mentorship Program Launches

We're excited to announce the launch of our new Brotherhood Mentorship Program, pairing upperclassman brothers with underclassmen to provide academic, professional, and personal guidance throughout their college journey.

Chapter News • November 8, 2025

Scholarship Recipients Announced

Congratulations to Brothers Marcus Johnson and David Williams for receiving the Kappa Chi Academic Excellence Scholarships. Both brothers maintained GPAs above 3.8 while contributing significantly to chapter activities.

Published October 20, 2025

Record Alumni Turnout for Homecoming Weekend

Over 80 alumni brothers returned to campus for our annual Homecoming celebration, making it our largest alumni gathering in chapter history. The weekend featured a golf tournament, awards banquet, and football tailgate.

Founder's Day Celebration Honors Legacy

The chapter hosted a special Founder's Day ceremony recognizing the 111th anniversary of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc. Brother Eugene Carter delivered an inspiring keynote on the importance of continuing our founders' vision.

Welcome Week Success: 47 Prospective Members

Our Fall 2025 Rush Week activities attracted 47 prospective members to learn about Phi Beta Sigma and the Kappa Chi Chapter. Information sessions, community service projects, and social events showcased our commitment to Brotherhood, Scholarship, and Service.
